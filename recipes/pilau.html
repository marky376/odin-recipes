Instructions

  1. Boil the water in a sufuria
  2. Gradually add the rice while stirring
  3. Stir until the mixture is thick and smooth
  4. Reduce the heat and let it simmer for 10 minutes
  5. Remove from heat and let it cool
  6. Serve with your favorite stew
